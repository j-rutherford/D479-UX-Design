Getting to Taniti

Plan Your Journey to Taniti

Taniti is well-connected by air and sea, making it easy to reach our beautiful island. Whether you're flying in or arriving by ferry, we have all the information you need to plan your trip.

Flights to Taniti

Taniti is served by several major airlines, with direct flights from major cities around the world. The island's modern airport is just a short drive from the main tourist areas, making your arrival smooth and convenient.

  • Direct Flights from Major Cities
  • Short Transfer to Main Attractions
  • Car Rentals and Shuttle Services Available at the Airport

Ferries to Taniti

If you prefer to travel by sea, regular ferry services connect Taniti with neighboring islands and the mainland. The ferry terminal is conveniently located near the main tourist areas, offering easy access to your accommodation.

  • Regular Services from Neighboring Islands
  • Scenic Views During Your Journey
  • Easy Access to Accommodations and Attractions

Transportation on the Island

Once you've arrived on Taniti, you'll find a variety of transportation options to explore the island. From car rentals to public transit, getting around is easy and convenient.

  • Car Rentals and Scooters
  • Public Buses and Taxis
  • Guided Tours with Transportation Included
