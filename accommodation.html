Where to Stay in Taniti

From luxury resorts to cozy beachside cottages, Taniti offers a variety of accommodations to suit every traveler's needs. Explore our top recommendations for a comfortable and memorable stay.

Luxury Resorts

Experience the epitome of luxury at Taniti's world-class resorts. With stunning ocean views, five-star amenities, and personalized service, our luxury resorts are perfect for those seeking an indulgent getaway.

  • Private Pools
  • On-site Spa and Wellness Centers

Beachside Cottages

Enjoy the charm and tranquility of Taniti's beachside cottages. Nestled along the shore, these cozy accommodations offer direct access to the beach, making them ideal for a peaceful retreat.

For those seeking privacy and exclusivity, Taniti's private villas offer the perfect setting. With expansive living spaces, private gardens, and personalized services, our villas are a home away from home.

Stay at one of Taniti's boutique hotels, where personalized service meets unique design. Each hotel offers a distinct experience, blending local culture with modern amenities.

  • Individually Designed Rooms

  • Personalized Guest Services
