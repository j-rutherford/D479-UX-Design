Things to Do

Experience the Best of Taniti

From thrilling water sports to immersive cultural experiences, Taniti offers a wide range of activities that cater to every interest. Discover the best ways to make the most of your stay on our beautiful island.

Water Sports

Take advantage of Taniti's crystal-clear waters with a variety of water sports. Whether you're into adrenaline-pumping activities like jet skiing and parasailing or prefer a more relaxed approach like kayaking or paddleboarding, there's something for everyone.

  • Jet Skiing and Parasailing
  • Snorkeling and Scuba Diving
  • Kayaking and Paddleboarding

Guided Tours

Explore Taniti's hidden gems with expert guides who will take you off the beaten path. From hiking through lush rainforests to exploring historical sites, these tours offer an in-depth look at the island's natural beauty and cultural heritage.

  • Rainforest Hiking Tours
  • Historical Site Explorations
  • Wildlife Observation Tours

Cultural Workshops

Immerse yourself in Taniti's rich culture by participating in hands-on workshops. Learn traditional crafts, try your hand at local cooking, or join a dance class to experience the island's vibrant traditions first-hand.

  • Traditional Craft Workshops
  • Local Cooking Classes
  • Island Dance Lessons

Nightlife

As the sun sets, Taniti comes alive with vibrant nightlife. Enjoy live music at beach bars, dance the night away at island clubs, or relax with a cocktail under the stars. There's no shortage of options for a fun and lively evening.

  • Beach Bars with Live Music
  • Island Nightclubs
  • Moonlit Beach Parties
